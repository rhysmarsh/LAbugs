Status classification criteria (iNaturalist observation frequency, LA County, place_id=962):
Common >200 obs/year; regularly encountered in habitat. Uncommon 20–200 obs/year; present but requires searching. Rare <20 obs/year; range edge or genuinely scarce. Endangered ESA/CESA/IUCN-listed. Vagrant Non-breeding irregular visitor. Extirpated Formerly present. Historical Not documented recently.

Tile indicators: Status badges appear on all tiles except Common. ✦ after the scientific name = introduced/non-native species. ⚠ INV = invasive species causing ecological or economic harm.
